### 2. **长难句分析**

- **句子1**: "Trying to look like the magazine pictures can take us on a long ride away from what beauty is really about."
  - **语法结构**: 
    - 主语: Trying to look like the magazine pictures
    - 谓语: can take
    - 宾语: us
    - 补语: on a long ride away from what beauty is really about
  - **拆解**:
    - "Trying to look like the magazine pictures" 是动名词短语作主语。
    - "can take us on a long ride" 是谓语部分，表示“可能会带我们走上一条漫长的路”。
    - "away from what beauty is really about" 是介词短语作补语，解释了这条路的方向，即远离真正的美。

- **句子2**: "While our initial attraction may be based in part on physical chemistry, it is the meeting of our inner worlds that creates the lasting intimacy and spiritual connection for which we long."
  - **语法结构**:
    - 主句: it is the meeting of our inner worlds that creates the lasting intimacy and spiritual connection for which we long.
    - 从句: While our initial attraction may be based in part on physical chemistry
  - **拆解**:
    - "While our initial attraction may be based in part on physical chemistry" 是让步状语从句，表示“尽管我们的最初吸引可能部分基于身体上的化学反应”。
    - "it is the meeting of our inner worlds that creates the lasting intimacy and spiritual connection for which we long" 是强调句型，强调“是我们的内心世界的交汇创造了我们渴望的持久的亲密和精神联系”。
